--------------------------
Blue Technologies

Antimass Deflectors
Vanilla
Requires: None
All of your ships may now move through Asteroid Fields but may never end their movement in an Asteroid Field.

Gravity Drive
Shattered Empire
Requires: Antimass Deflectors
You do not need to roll for your ships when moving out of a Gravity Rift. If your ships begin their activation in a system adjacent to a Gravity Rift or Wormhole, you receive +1 movement.

XRD Transporters
Vanilla
Requires: Antimass Deflectors
All of your Carriers now receive +1 movement.

Light/Wave Deflector
Vanilla
Requires: XRD Transporters AND Magen Defense Grid
Your ships may now move through systems containing enemy ships and continue their movement to the activated system.

Maneuvering Jets
Shattered Empire
Requires: XRD Transporters
Your opponents receive -1 to all PDS rolls targeting your ships (or -2 if the PDS is firing from an adjacent system). In addition, you receive -1 to all your Space Mine rolls, and your ships do not have to stop when entering an Ion Storm.

Type IV Drive
Vanilla
Requires: XRD Transporter AND Neural Motivator
All of your Cruisers and Dreadnoughts now receive +1 movement.

Advanced Fighters
Shattered Empire
Requires: Type IV Drive
Your Fighters do not require the support of Carriers or Space Docks, may move independently with a movement rate of 2, and receive +1 on combat rolls. Enemy ships may not move through a system your Fighters occupy. Fighters in excess of a system's Fighter capacity count towards your Fleet Supply limit.

Fleet Logistics
Vanilla
Requires: Graviton Negator
When taking a Tactical Action, you may now take two Tactical Actions, one after the other, before your turn ends.

Green Technologies

Stasis Capsules
Vanilla
Requires: Enviro Compensator
Your Cruisers and Dreadnoughts may now carry one Ground Force unit.

Cybernetics
Vanilla
Requires: Stasis Capsules OR Antimass Deflectors
All of your Fighters now receive +1 on all combat rolls.

Neural Motivator
Vanilla
Requires: Stasis Capsules OR Micro Technology
You now draw one extra Action Card during each Status Phase.

Dacxive Animators
Vanilla
Requires: Neural Motivator
If you win an Invasion Combat, roll once for every Ground Force unit killed (yours and your opponent's). For every roll of 6+, place one Ground Force unit on the planet from your reinforcements.

Gen Synthesis
Vanilla
Requires: Cybernetics
All of your Ground Forces now receive +1 on all combat rolls during Invasion Combat. When one of your Ground Force units is destroyed, roll a die: On a result of 5+, the unit is instead returned to a planet in your Home System.

Neural Computing
Shards of the Throne
Requires: Neural Motivator
Your cost to purchase new Technologies is reduced by 2.

Hyper Metabolism
Shattered Empire
Requires: Gen Synthesis
During the Status Phase, you gain 1 additional Command Counter. In addition, before drawing Action Cards, you may discard 1 Action Card from your hand to draw 1 additional card.

X-89 Bacterial Weapon
Vanilla
Requires: Transit Diodes OR Assault Cannon
Your Dreadnought or War Sun units may now use this option before bombarding. Immediately destroy all enemy Ground Forces on the planet. Then discard all of your Action Cards.
--------------------------
Yellow Technologies

Enviro Compensator
Vanilla
Requires: None
The production capacity of your Space Docks is now increased by 1.

Transfabrication
Shards of the throne
Requires: None
You may scuttle units at the start of the Build Units step of your activation. For each non-Fighter and non-Ground force unit you scuttle in this way, gain 1 Trade Good.

Sarween Tools
Vanilla
Requires: Enviro Compensator
Whenever you produce units at any Space Dock, you now receive one additional resource with which to build units.

Graviton Laser System
Vanilla
Requires: Deep Space Cannon
When you make a combat roll with a PDS unit and miss, you may now make a single re-roll for each roll that missed.

Micro Technology
Shattered Empire
Requires: Stasis Capsules OR Sarween Tools
When you receive Trade Goods from your trade agreements, you now receive one additional Trade Good for each of your active trade agreements.

Integrated Economy
Vanilla
Requires: Micro Technology AND Cybernetics
When producing units at your Space Docks, you may now place the new units in any activated, adjacent system that is empty or friendly. You may place PDS and Ground Force units on any friendly planet within this range.

Nano Technology
Shattered Empire
Requires: Micro Technology
Your Dreadnoughts and War Suns may not be targeted by your opponents' Action Cards. In addition, when you claim a planet, you gain its planet card refreshed.

Transit Diodes
Vanilla
Requires: Light/Wave Deflector OR Dacxive Animators
As an action, you may spend one Command Counter from your Strategic Allocation to immediately move up to four of your Ground Forces from any one of your planets to any other planet you control.

Red Technologies

Hylar V Assault Laser
Vanilla
Requires: None
All of your Cruisers and Destroyers now receive +1 on all combat rolls.

Automated Defense Turrets
Shattered Empire
Requires: Hylar V Assault Laser
All of your Destroyers receive +2 and roll 1 additional die on all Anti-Fighter Barrage rolls.

Deep Space Cannon
Vanilla
Requires: Hylar V Assault Laser
Enemy fleets in adjacent systems are now in range of your PDS units.

Duranium Armor
Shards of the Throne
Requires: Deep Space Cannon
At the end of each round of combat, you may repair 1 of your damaged units in the battle.

Magen Defense Grid
Vanilla
Requires: Deep Space Cannon
All of your PDS units now receive +1 on all combat rolls. In addition, all of your defending Ground Forces on a planet with a PDS receive +1 on all combat rolls during Invasion Combat.

War Sun
Vanilla
Requires: Deep Space Cannon AND Sarween Tools
You are now allowed to produce War Sun units.

Assault Cannon
Shattered Empire
Requires: Cybernetics AND Automated Defense Turrets
Before any Space Battle begins, your participating Dreadnoughts may each fire one shot. Any hits are applied immediately, and casualties do not receive return fire.

Graviton Negator
Vanilla
Requires: Assault Cannon OR Dacxive Animators
Your Dreadnoughts may now bombard planets that contain PDS units. Your Fighters may participate in Invasion Combat. Surviving Fighters are returned to space after the combat and can never establish control of a planet.
