Monetary Concession
"What do you desire? Money? If you support me, I'll drown you in riches!"
Play as an action: You may force me to give you all my Trade Goods.

Promise of Protection
"My spies and assassins are at your disposal."
Play after I target a Representative with a Spy: You may force me to target a different Representative.

Support of the Throne
"Support me today, and I will support your claim for the galaxy!"
You require 1 less victory point in order to win the game.

Territorial Concession
"I'll repay your loyalty with any system of your choice... and all its inhabitants."
At the start of a combat round in Space Battle:
You may force me to retreat from the battle.

Voting Concession
"You know you're going to need my support in the future. I promise to be your pawn if you support me."
Play during the Bargaining step of a Council: You may force me to vote in your favor.
